HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF FROM CORONA VIRUS INFECTION

Wash your hands with soap and water for 40 seconds or use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer for 20 seconds

Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing using a tissue or your elbow and by wearing a cloth mask whenever you are going out

Avoid close contact with those having respiratory symptoms such as coughing or sneezing

HOME QUARANTINE PROCESS

  • Cover your coughs and sneezes with your bent elbow or by using tissue.
  • Wash your hands with soap and water for 40 seconds or rub them using an alcohol-based sanitizer for 20 seconds
  • Throw used tissues in the trash
  • Stay home in a room and away from others
  • Let those around you help you and look after you
  • If you have to contact directly with others, wear a cloth mask when leaving the hosue or going out in the public
