HOW TO STAY SAFE AND PROTECT YOURSELF FROM CORONA VIRUS

General Guidelines :

-> Avoid close contact with anyone and call 999 when you have been in contact with infected people or have any of the following symptoms

  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Difficulty breathing

-> Wear a cloth mask

-> Avoid handshakes and hugs

-> Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth before washing your hands

-> Keep a safe distance of at least 2 meters

-> Avoid gatherings or crowds

-> Do not share personal items with others

-> Make sure your hands are clean by :

  • Cough
  • Washing your hands with soap and water for 40 seconds
  • or use alcohol-based hand sanitizer for 20 seconds

What to do when going out?

  • Do not leave the house unless necessary
  • Bring an additional cloth mask
  • Bring alcohol-based hand sanitizer

What to do when going to work?

  • Avoid handshakes and hugs
  • Prevent gathering in groups
  • Limit in-person meetings
  • Reduce number of attendees in meetings
  • Immediately leave the workplace at your scheduled time
  • Clean and disinfect all surfaces
  • Make sure meeting rooms are well-ventilated
  • Train cleaning workers on proper cleaning and disinfection practices

What to do when going shopping?

  • Do not take those at higher risk of devloping serious symptoms if infected
  • Bring an additional cloth mask
  • Bring alcohol-based hand sanitizer
  • Disinfect your hands when entering and leaving shopping stores
  • Measure your temperature at entrances and respong to any directions
  • Disinfect the handles of your cart or basket before using it
  • Use the stairs instead of the elevator
  • Leave 6 steps between yourself and others on the escelator
  • Pay electronically
  • Disinfect and clean goods before consumption

What to do when receving orders and deliveries?

  • Check the driver or shipping company are delivering your order
  • Avoid receiving your order directly
  • Pay for your order electronically
  • Remove and dispose of unnecessary packaging
  • Disinfect the solid surfaces of the items
  • Store properly
  • Wash fruits and vegetables thoroughly
